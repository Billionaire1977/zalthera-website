Use Case

Deviations are often documented via e-mail or informal processes. ZAL-Gate replaces that with an identity-bound, scoped and timeboxed release workflow — fully auditable.

  • Release decision with reason + scope (lot/parts/time window)
  • Evidence attachment (photo/doc) and audit log
  • Exportable record for compliance & traceability

What it is

Process & release governance. Proof instead of assumptions.

What it is not

No employee monitoring, no behavior tracking, no hidden scoring.

Boundary

No autonomous actions. No machine control. No parameter changes.
